Diabetes Prediction Inputs in Short

Provided following are the details to predict the Diabetes Condition:
  • Age: The age of the individual.

  • Smoking History: The smoking history of the individual.Example: never, No Info, current, former, not current

  • BMI: The Body Mass Index (BMI) of the individual. Example: 27.32, 19.24, 36.38, 24.18, 25.84

  • HbA1C level: The Hemoglobin A1c level, a measure of blood sugar levels over the past 2-3 months. Example: 6.6, 4.8, 5, 9, 7.5, 5.7

  • Blood Glucose Level: The blood glucose level of the individual. Example: 100, 300, 200, 160, 85, 124, 235
